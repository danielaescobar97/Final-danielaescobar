D´KATTA

D´Katta is a project of artisanal and elite Colombian coffee from Manizales, Caldas. The name D´Katta refers to an elite coffee intended for tasting. The logo design is inspired by the idea of ​​bringing the rural environment to your home, represented by a window shape and plantations with coffee beans and a coffee cup. In addition, the packaging features hand-drawn illustrations of typical birds from the region, adding an authentic and local touch.
